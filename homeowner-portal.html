🔄 Changing your air filter

Your AC filter catches dust, pollen, pet dander, and other particles before they circulate through your home. A clogged filter makes your system work harder, uses more energy, and shortens the life of your unit.

  • Change your filter every 90 days as a baseline
  • If you have pets or allergies, change it every 30–60 days
  • Use a MERV 8–11 rated filter for best balance of airflow and filtration
  • Check the filter size printed on the old one before buying a replacement
  • A dirty filter will cause your unit to freeze up — if you see ice on your lines, check the filter first

🌡️ Setting your thermostat

In South Florida's heat and humidity, the right thermostat setting makes a big difference in your energy bill and comfort level.

  • Set to 78°F when home — optimal comfort vs. energy cost
  • Set to 82°F when away — saves energy without stressing the system
  • Avoid setting below 72°F — causes the system to over-run and can freeze the coils
  • Use a programmable or smart thermostat to automate your schedule
  • Keep interior doors open for better airflow throughout the home

🧹 Clearing your outdoor unit

Your outdoor condenser unit needs airflow to release heat. Blocked airflow causes it to overheat and work inefficiently.

  • Keep at least 2 feet of clearance around all sides of the unit
  • Remove leaves, grass clippings, and debris monthly
  • Trim any shrubs or plants growing near the unit
  • Gently rinse the fins with a garden hose once a season — spray from inside out
  • Never cover the unit while it's running or during summer months

📅 Annual tune-up

Even a new system benefits from a yearly professional checkup. In South Florida, schedule before summer (ideally March–April) to beat the rush.

  • A technician will check refrigerant levels, electrical connections, and drain lines
  • They'll clean the evaporator and condenser coils for maximum efficiency
  • Your warranty may require annual maintenance — check your paperwork
  • Regular tune-ups extend your system's life by 3–5 years on average
  • Book early — May and June have 6–8 week wait times in South Florida
