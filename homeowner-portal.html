Changing your air filter

A clogged filter makes your system work harder, uses more energy, and shortens its life.

  • Change every 90 days as a baseline
  • With pets or allergies, change every 30–60 days
  • Use MERV 8–11 rated filters
  • Check the size printed on the old filter before buying a replacement
  • A dirty filter causes the unit to freeze — if you see ice on the lines, check the filter first

Setting your thermostat

In South Florida's heat and humidity, the right temperature setting makes a big difference.

  • Set to 78°F when home — optimal balance of comfort and savings
  • Set to 82°F when away — saves energy without stressing the system
  • Avoid going below 72°F — can freeze the coils
  • A smart thermostat can automate this schedule for you
← All tips

Clearing your outdoor unit

Your outdoor condenser needs airflow to release heat efficiently.

  • Keep at least 2 feet of clearance around all sides
  • Remove leaves, grass clippings, and debris monthly
  • Trim any shrubs or plants growing near the unit
  • Gently rinse the fins with a garden hose once a season
← All tips

Annual tune-up

Book March–April to beat the South Florida summer rush.

  • Technician checks refrigerant levels, electrical connections, and drain lines
  • Coils are cleaned for maximum efficiency
  • Your warranty may require annual maintenance — check your paperwork
  • Regular tune-ups extend system life by 3–5 years
