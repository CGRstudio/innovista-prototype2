Common questions

Can I start on Basic and upgrade later?
Yes. Start free, get your first leads in, and upgrade to Standard or Pro when you're ready. Your data carries over instantly.
What makes the AI chatbot Pro-only?
The AI chatbot qualifies every lead automatically 24/7 — no human needed. It's the most powerful feature in the platform and the centerpiece of what makes Pro worth it.
Is there a setup fee?
No setup fees on any plan. Pro clients get a done-for-you onboarding and snapshot deployment within 24 hours of signing up.
What trades do you support?
Launching with roofing first, then HVAC, plumbing, electrical, and landscaping. Pro clients get early access to each new trade snapshot as it launches.
Can my clients log in and see their own data?
Yes. Every business owner gets their own dashboard. Their customers get a personal service portal for quotes, appointments, and maintenance tips.
