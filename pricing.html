Common questions

Can I start on Basic and upgrade later?
Absolutely. Start free, get your first leads in, and upgrade to Standard or Pro when you're ready to automate. Your data and leads carry over instantly.
What makes the AI chatbot Pro-only?
The AI chatbot qualifies every lead automatically — 24/7, no human needed. It's the most powerful feature in the platform and the centerpiece of what makes Pro worth it.
Is there a setup fee?
No setup fees on any plan. Pro clients get a done-for-you onboarding and snapshot deployment within 24 hours of signing up.
What trades do you support?
We're launching with roofing first, then expanding to HVAC, plumbing, electrical, and landscaping. Pro clients get early access to each new trade snapshot as it launches.
Can my clients have their own login?
Yes. Every business owner gets their own dashboard login. Their customers get a personal service portal to view quotes, appointments, and maintenance tips — no app download needed.
